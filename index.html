Passions & Pursuits

As an actor and junior executive based in Los Angeles, my life is a blend of creativity and business. Here's a glimpse into the various facets that shape my world both on and off screen.

Film & TV

My passion for storytelling comes alive through acting and producing. From indie projects to mainstream productions, I'm always seeking new ways to contribute to the entertainment industry.

Physical Fitness

Maintaining peak physical condition is crucial for my roles and overall well-being. I'm dedicated to a rigorous fitness regimen that keeps me camera-ready and energized for long days on set.

Business Acumen

As a junior executive, I'm constantly honing my business skills. I apply strategic thinking and leadership abilities to navigate the complex landscape of the entertainment industry.

World Traveler

Exploring new cultures and locations is not just a hobby, but a source of inspiration for my work. Each journey broadens my perspective and enriches my performances.

Golf Enthusiast

On the green, I find both relaxation and challenge. Golf teaches patience and precision, skills that translate well into my professional life in acting and business.

Behind the Scenes

My interest in producing allows me to see projects from conception to completion. I'm fascinated by the collaborative process of bringing stories to life on screen.

Continuous Learning

Whether it's a new script or a business strategy, I'm always eager to learn. This curiosity drives my growth as both an actor and an executive.

Networking

Building and maintaining relationships is key in the entertainment industry. I value the connections I've made and always look forward to meeting new collaborators.

Character Study

Delving deep into character analysis is one of my favorite aspects of acting. I enjoy the challenge of bringing complex personalities to life on screen.

LA Living

Los Angeles isn't just my home; it's my playground and workplace. I'm constantly inspired by the city's energy, diversity, and endless opportunities in the entertainment world.

Advocacy

I'm passionate about using my platform to advocate for causes I believe in, particularly those related to the arts and youth empowerment in the entertainment industry.

Innovation

In both acting and business, I'm always looking for innovative approaches. I believe in pushing boundaries and exploring new technologies that can enhance storytelling and industry practices.
